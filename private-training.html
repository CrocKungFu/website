Private Training

Personal instruction tailored to your goals, experience and interests.

Whether you’re seeking health and wellbeing, martial arts training, or a deeper understanding of the arts, private sessions provide dedicated guidance and individual attention.

Train at your own pace and explore the aspects of practice that matter most to you.

Why Choose Private Training?

Personal Guidance

Receive individual instruction tailored to your goals, experience and interests. Whether you’re completely new to the arts or looking to refine existing skills, training is adapted to your needs.

Learn At Your Own Pace

Private sessions allow you to progress without the pace or structure of a group class. Take the time to explore movements, techniques and principles with focused attention and clear guidance.

Go Deeper

Explore specific aspects of Kung Fu, Chi Kung or Shuang Yang in greater depth. Private training creates space for questions, refinement and a more personal learning experience.

What Makes Private Training Different?

Personal Guidance

Receive individual instruction tailored to your needs, experience and goals. Whether you’re completely new to the arts or looking to refine existing skills, training is adapted to support your personal development.

Flexible Scheduling

Private sessions can be arranged around your availability and individual circumstances. This creates greater flexibility for those whose schedule, travel plans or commitments make regular classes difficult to attend.

Faster Progress

With direct feedback and focused attention throughout the session, private training often provides the quickest path to improvement. Every session is designed to help you make the most of your training time.

Who Is It For?

Complete Beginners

No previous experience is required. Private training provides a comfortable environment to learn at your own pace, ask questions and build confidence without the pressure of keeping up with a group.

Existing Students

For those already training, private sessions provide the opportunity to refine techniques, improve forms and explore specific areas of practice in greater detail.

Visitors To Corfu

Many visitors use private sessions as a way to experience the arts during their stay on the island. Whether you’re here for a few days or a few weeks, training can be arranged around your schedule and interests.

Couples, Families & Small Groups

Private sessions can also be arranged for couples, families and small groups. The first question is always the same: what do you want people to experience? From there, the session is built around the people, the venue and the purpose of the gathering.
